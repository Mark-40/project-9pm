## 1. What is Cryptocurrency?

Think of cryptocurrency like **digital money** that exists only on the internet — there are no physical coins or bills.

The most famous one is **Bitcoin (BTC)**. Others include Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and more.

### Simple Analogy

> Imagine buying and selling **collectible trading cards**. Some days the cards are worth more, some days less. If you buy when the price is low and sell when the price is high — you make money. Crypto works the same way, just digitally and much faster.

### What is USDT?

**USDT (Tether)** is a special crypto that is always equal to **1 US Dollar**. This app only trades against USDT pairs, meaning you always know the price in dollars.

```
BTC/USDT = "How many US Dollars is 1 Bitcoin worth right now?"
ETH/USDT = "How many US Dollars is 1 Ethereum worth right now?"
```

## 2. What is Crypto Trading?

Trading means **buying crypto when you think the price will go up**, and **selling when you think it will go down** — so you profit from the difference.

### Example

```
You buy 1 SOL at $150
Price goes up to $157
You sell at $157
Profit = $7 (about ₱400)
```

### The Problem

Nobody knows for certain if the price will go up or down. That's where **tools and analysis** come in — and that's exactly what this app provides.

## 7. What the Signals Mean

The app generates three possible signals:

### 🟢 BUY
> The analysis suggests the price is likely to go **up**. This is a potential opportunity to **enter a position** (buy the coin).

### 🔴 SELL
> The analysis suggests the price is likely to go **down**. This is a potential signal to **exit a position** (sell the coin you hold).

### 🟡 HOLD
> The analysis is **not conclusive**. No strong signal in either direction. Best to **wait** and not make any move.

---

### Signal Quality Score (0–100)

Not all BUY/SELL signals are equal. The app scores each signal:

| Score | Label | What it means | Should you act? |
|-------|-------|---------------|-----------------|
| 75–100 | ⭐ Very Strong | Multiple indicators agree strongly | High confidence |
| 50–74 | Strong | Good alignment across indicators | Worth considering |
| 25–49 | Moderate | Partial agreement | Be cautious |
| 0–24 | Weak | Indicators are mixed | Best to skip |

> **Tip:** Only act on signals with a score of **50 or above** to improve your odds.

---

### Confluence Indicator

This tells you if the **shorter 15-minute chart** agrees with the **1-hour signal**.

| Confluence | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| ✅ 15m Aligned ▲ | Both 15min and 1hr agree it's going up — stronger BUY |
| ✅ 15m Aligned ▼ | Both 15min and 1hr agree it's going down — stronger SELL |
| ⚠️ 15m Mixed | Short-term and long-term disagree — be more cautious |

Think of it like asking two different doctors the same question. If both agree — you're more confident in the diagnosis.

## 8. The App's Brain — How Signals Are Generated

The app uses **two independent systems** that cross-check each other:

### System 1 — Rule-Based Technical Analysis (TA)

This is like a **checklist** of 5 market indicators. Each indicator votes BUY or SELL:

| Indicator | What it measures | BUY signal | SELL signal |
|-----------|-----------------|------------|-------------|
| **RSI** (Relative Strength Index) | Is the coin oversold or overbought? | RSI below 35 (oversold = cheap) | RSI above 65 (overbought = expensive) |
| **MACD** | Is momentum shifting upward or downward? | MACD crossing above signal line | MACD crossing below signal line |
| **Bollinger Bands** | Is price outside its normal range? | Price below lower band | Price above upper band |
| **EMA Cross** | Short-term trend vs long-term trend | 9-period EMA above 21-period EMA | 9-period EMA below 21-period EMA |
| **Stochastic** | Is the coin in extreme territory? | Stochastic below 25 | Stochastic above 75 |

If **3 or more indicators vote the same way** → Signal is generated.

---

### System 2 — AI Machine Learning Model

The app also runs an **XGBoost AI model** (the same type of AI used by banks and hedge funds) that was trained on historical price data.

The AI has learned patterns from **1,000 hours** of historical data per coin and outputs:
- **BUY probability** (e.g., 72% chance price goes up)
- **SELL probability**
- **HOLD probability**

If the AI confidence is **60% or above**, it overrides the rule-based system.

### How They Work Together

```
Rule-Based TA  ──┐
                 ├──→  Final Signal (BUY / SELL / HOLD)
AI ML Model    ──┘

If AI confidence >= 60%: AI wins
If AI confidence < 60%:  TA rules win
```

## 9. Key Features Explained

### 📊 Backtest (Historical Testing)

Before risking real money, you can see **how the strategy would have performed** on historical data.

Click the **Backtest** tab and press **Run Backtest** for any coin.

> ⚠️ **Important:** Past performance does not guarantee future results. Backtest is a guide, not a guarantee.

## 11. Risks You Must Know

We believe in full transparency. Here are the real risks:

### ⚠️ Market Risk
Crypto markets are **highly volatile**. Prices can drop 20–30% in a single day during crashes. No signal system can predict black swan events (sudden crashes, regulatory news, exchange hacks).

### ⚠️ Signal Accuracy
The app is correct approximately **50–55% of the time** in typical conditions. This means roughly **1 in 2 trades may lose money**. A higher win rate requires careful filtering (quality score + confluence).

### ⚠️ No Guarantee of Profit
Past backtest performance **does not guarantee future results**. Markets change. A strategy that worked for 6 months may underperform in the next 6 months.

### ⚠️ This App Does Not Trade For You
The app generates signals and shows you information. **You make the final decision** to buy or sell. The app is a decision-support tool, not an automated trading bot.

### ⚠️ Only Invest What You Can Afford to Lose
Never put in money you need for rent, food, or bills. Treat crypto trading money as money you are comfortable losing entirely.
